## Transfiguration (S13/LoH Horadric Cube endgame craft)
- Applied to a FINISHED item (tempered+masterworked+aspected): ONE random powerful modification; item
  usually becomes permanently UNMODIFIABLE (small chance to stay modifiable → double transfiguration).
- Outcome pool: bonus stats · extra Greater Affix · gem-strength bonuses · +ranks to a skill CATEGORY
  (+2–3 default; +4–6 Ultimate ranks on 2H) · Indestructible · Replace-Affix.
- **Tuning Prisms** steer outcomes (Entropic Prism = guaranteed useful outcome; removes
  Indestructible/Replace-Affix/Greater-Affix outcomes; always unmodifiable).
- **Vessel model:** optional `transfigured: { outcomeType, affix?, values? }` on the BUILD item row (a
  per-item roll, not catalog content). Maxroll planner field not observed on sampled builds — capture the
  encoding from the first transfigured build we import (Mobalytics calls it `transfiguredStats`). verify-live.
